--- layout: about profile: align: right image: profile.jpg published: true --- I am an assistant professor in the School of Aerospace Engineering and a member of the Institute for Robotics and Intelligent Machines (IRIM) at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I was a postdoctoral scholar at ETH Zurich. I completed my PhD in Aeronautics and Astronautics at the University of Washington, advised by Behçet Açıkmeşe and Pierre-Loïc Garoche.
I am interested in improving the coordination and control of interacting dynamical systems with an emphasis on optimality, scalability, and robustness. My research combines techniques from optimization, control theory, and game theory to develop models and algorithms for applications including urban transportation, advanced air mobility, and space collision avoidance.
